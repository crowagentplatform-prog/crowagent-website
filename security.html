Enterprise-grade security

Security at CrowAgent

Your compliance data deserves enterprise-grade protection. CrowAgent is built with security, privacy, and resilience at its core — so you can trust every report, every time.

AES-256 encryption

All data encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. UK data residency by default — your data never leaves UK-hosted infrastructure.

GDPR compliant

Full data processing agreements, right to erasure, and data portability. ICO registered. Privacy-first architecture with data minimisation at every layer.

ISO 27001 aligned

Security controls aligned to ISO 27001 Annex A. Our information security management practices follow the ISO 27001 framework to protect your data systematically.

99.9% uptime SLA

Enterprise-grade infrastructure on UK cloud. Redundant architecture with automated failover ensures your compliance tools are available when you need them.

Responsible AI

CrowAgent uses AI responsibly and transparently. Here is how we handle AI in our platform:

  • Gemini AI is used server-side only for narrative generation — never in the browser
  • No client-side AI processing — all inference runs on secured backend infrastructure
  • All AI outputs are auditable and traceable to their source inputs
  • No training on customer data — your data is never used to improve AI models

Infrastructure & trust

Multiple layers of protection safeguard your data and ensure platform reliability.

Cloudflare protection

DDoS protection, Bot Fight Mode, and SSL Full Strict mode on all endpoints. Every request is inspected and filtered before reaching our servers.

Row Level Security

Supabase Row Level Security enforced on all database tables. Every query is scoped to the authenticated user — no cross-tenant data leakage is possible.

Regular security assessments

Ongoing security assessments and vulnerability monitoring across our infrastructure. Dependencies are audited and patched on a continuous basis.

Responsible disclosure

Found a vulnerability? We welcome responsible disclosure. Contact us at security@crowagent.ai and we will respond within 48 hours.
