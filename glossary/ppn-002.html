DEFINED TERM

PPN 002 (Procurement Policy Note 002)

UK Cabinet Office policy setting a mandatory minimum 10% social value weighting on central government contract evaluations.

Definition

Procurement Policy Note 002 (February 2025) is a UK Cabinet Office instruction requiring all central government departments, their executive agencies, and non-departmental public bodies to allocate a minimum 10% weighting of the total contract evaluation score to social value. PPN 002 applies to new procurements; contracts initiated under PPN 06/20 continue to operate under the earlier framework.

The 5 PPN 002 missions

  • 1. Fighting climate change: carbon reduction, waste minimisation, sustainable procurement
  • 2. Tackling economic inequality: SME supply-chain access, skills development, fair work
  • 3. Equal opportunity: inclusive employment, disability representation, progression pathways
  • 4. Wellbeing: community integration, mental health, workforce wellbeing
  • 5. Covid-19 recovery: (legacy mission, reducing in relevance as recovery progresses)

Key facts

  • Minimum weighting: 10% of total evaluation score (buyers can set higher)
  • Scoring framework: TOMs (Themes, Outcomes, Measures)
  • Proxy values: Oxford Social Value Bank 2023–24 is the canonical reference
  • Scope: new central government contracts from February 2025
  • Relation to PPN 06/20: PPN 002 builds on and supersedes PPN 06/20 (September 2020) for new contracts; PPN 06/20 continues to apply to contracts initiated under its regime
