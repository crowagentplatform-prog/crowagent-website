1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We also use similar technologies such as local storage tokens. This policy explains what we use, why, and how you can control them, in compliance with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

5. How to Opt Out
