Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We also use similar technologies such as local storage tokens. This policy explains what we use, why, and how you can control them, in compliance with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR.

Sentry captures JavaScript errors and performance issues to help us fix bugs. Sentry may set session identifiers in browser memory. This processing is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining a stable, secure platform.

5. How to Opt Out

  • Cookie consent banner: click “Manage cookies” in the footer or re-open the banner at any time to change your analytics preferences.
  • Browser settings: you can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in.
  • PostHog opt-out: visit PostHog opt-out page.

6. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy when we add or remove cookies. Material changes will be notified via our cookie consent banner.
