ABOUT

Compliance software built for UK organisations

We build software that helps UK organisations handle MEES, PPN 002, CSRD, and related compliance requirements. Our workflows follow the source regulations directly, so you get answers you can stand behind.

Our mission

To make sustainability compliance accessible, affordable, and verifiable for every UK organisation, regardless of size or budget.

What we do

We build tools that read the actual regulations: MEES penalty schedules, PPN 002 social value models, and CSRD reporting thresholds. We turn them into clear, actionable workflows. No guesswork, no generic templates.

Our Approach

Every feature we build starts with the actual regulation: SI 2015/962 for energy efficiency, PPN 002 for social value, EU 2022/2464 for sustainability reporting. We read the source legislation, then build workflows that follow it step by step, so you get answers you can trust.
