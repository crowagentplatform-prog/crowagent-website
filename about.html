Compliance software built for

CrowAgent builds software that helps UK organisations work through MEES, PPN 002, CSRD, and related compliance requirements using workflows grounded in the source regulations, not generic consulting advice.

To make sustainability compliance accessible, affordable, and verifiable for every UK organisation — not just those who can afford consultants.

Our Approach

We don't believe compliance needs to be complicated. CrowAgent combines AI intelligence with regulatory expertise to deliver answers in minutes, not months. Every feature we build is grounded in actual UK regulation — SI 2015/962 for energy efficiency, PPN 002 for social value, EU 2022/2464 for sustainability reporting.
