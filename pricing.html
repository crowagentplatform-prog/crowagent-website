Frequently Asked Questions

What is the entry-level plan?
CrowAgent Core Starter is £149/mo and includes up to 5 properties. Start with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
How does annual billing work?
Pay upfront for the year and save 20% compared to monthly billing. To switch to annual billing, contact hello@crowagent.ai.
What happens when my trial ends?
After your 14-day trial you'll be moved to your chosen paid plan. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends.
Do you offer enterprise pricing?
Yes, we offer custom enterprise packages tailored to your needs. Contact hello@crowagent.ai for a bespoke quote.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is protected with AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit. We are fully GDPR compliant with UK data residency.
