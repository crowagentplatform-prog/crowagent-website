FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. CrowAgent Core is also free during the active pilot phase.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

How does annual billing work?

Choose annual billing to save 20% compared to monthly pricing. You pay upfront for the year and your subscription renews automatically. Contact us at hello@crowagent.ai to switch to annual billing.

What if I need more than 100 properties or custom requirements?

We offer bespoke enterprise plans for large portfolios and organisations with specific compliance needs. Get in touch and we will put together a tailored package for your organisation.
