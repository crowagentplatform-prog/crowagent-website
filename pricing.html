Frequently asked questions

Is there a free tier?

The CSRD Checker is free and does not require an account. CrowAgent Core and CrowMark are paid products with a 14-day trial.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can move to a different plan from billing once your account is live. We are also tightening the in-app billing flow so plan changes are clearer for existing customers.

How does annual billing work?

Pay upfront for the year and save 10% compared to monthly billing. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time.

What happens when my trial ends?

At the end of the 14-day period, you can choose whether to continue on a paid plan. You are not charged during the trial itself.

Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes. Enterprise plans start at £999/mo and include SSO, custom SLA, dedicated customer success, and bespoke integrations. Contact hello@crowagent.ai for a quote.

Is my data secure?

Yes. AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, UK data residency, GDPR compliant, ICO registered. See our security page for details.
