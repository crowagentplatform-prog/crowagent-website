Procurement Policy Note 002 (PPN 002) requires all central government contracting authorities to evaluate social value with a minimum 10% weighting on all contracts above the procurement threshold. Failure to demonstrate social value costs you contracts.

PPN 002 applies to central government. Devolved administrations and local authorities may apply the Social Value Act 2012 differently.
