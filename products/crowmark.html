FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is PPN 002?

Procurement Policy Note 002 (PPN 002) is a UK government policy that requires all central government contracts above £5 million to include a minimum 10% weighting for social value in the evaluation criteria. Many local authorities and NHS trusts apply the same standard voluntarily.

What are Oxford SVB proxy values?

The Oxford Social Value Bank provides monetised proxy values for social outcomes — for example, the value of one person gaining employment, or one apprenticeship being created. CrowMark uses the 2023-24 proxy values, the most current version.

What is the TOMs framework?

The National TOMs (Themes, Outcomes and Measures) Framework is the most widely used social value measurement methodology in UK public procurement. CrowMark aligns all measures to the TOMs framework automatically.

How does the 3 bids/month limit work on Solo?

Each new contract wizard submission counts as one bid. The limit resets at the start of each calendar month. Upgrade to Team for unlimited bids.

Can I use CrowMark for frameworks and call-offs?

Yes. CrowMark works for any PPN 002-relevant procurement — single contracts, framework agreements, and call-offs. Enter the relevant contract value and contracting authority type and CrowMark maps the appropriate missions.
