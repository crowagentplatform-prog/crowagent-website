Since 1 April 2023, it is unlawful to continue letting commercial property with EPC rating F or G (SI 2015/962). Proposed Band C target by April 2028 affects 400,000+ commercial properties. Penalties: £5,000–£150,000 per property (rateable-value based, SI 2015/962 regulation 39).

Band C 2028 is a proposed regulatory target subject to legislative confirmation. Not yet enacted law.
